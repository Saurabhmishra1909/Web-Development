Tribute to Device

Peter "Dupreeh" Rasmussen is a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player from Denmark. He is widely regarded as one of the best CSGO players in the world, and has won numerous championships and awards throughout his career.

Device is known for his incredible accuracy and consistency, and his ability to make clutch plays in high-pressure situations. He has played for some of the top teams in CSGO, including Astralis and Ninjas in Pyjamas, and has achieved great success at major tournaments such as the ELEAGUE Major and the ESL Pro League.

Off the battlefield, Device is known for his calm and humble demeanor, and is respected by his peers and fans alike. He is a true ambassador for the sport of CSGO, and has inspired countless players to pursue their dreams of becoming professional gamers.
